Overview

Please read and understand our Notice and Disclaimer before reading this document, please read our User Agreement before playing ChessX or affiliated products, and please note this document is subject to change as development progresses.
ChessX is free to play with no staking or wagering of money or money's worth.

ChessX Inc. teaches core life skills like logic, language, and math with puzzle games like chess, crosswords, and sudoku. We use blockchain technology to align the incentive to learn with economic opportunities for individuals through self-custody and delegation of virtual assets.

ChessX is a free to play mobile chess game for iOS and Android. Players earn Bitcoin rewards and NFT chess pieces by competing for treasure chests and solving puzzles to open them.

  • CHX is an in-game currency that incentivizes player liquidity and enables upgrades of in-game items.
  • LRN is a utility token spent to activate and upgrade Hero pieces. xLRN (staked LRN) is the governance token that grants access to premium ChessX Guild tools, as well as votes to modify economic incentives and govern the ChessX treasury.

Core Loop

Every meaningful action in ChessX earns Gems instantly. Gems are capped daily and weekly — no infinite farming.

Play & Earn Gems

ActionGems Earned
Castling+5 Gems
10-minute focused session+20 Gems

Gems → $LRN

At the end of each month, all unspent Gems auto-convert into $LRN and loyalty points.

$LRN → Value

Every fiat upgrade fee triggers an automatic buyback and burn of $LRN. Loyalty points from Gems can be staked for small $LRN yield or used to enter exclusive tournaments, creating continuous demand.

Chess cannot be "won" by cheating for rewards because rewards are engagement-based, not win-based.

How NFTs Support the Token

  • Secondary market royalty: 5% fee on every resale → 100% goes to automatic $LRN buyback & burn

  • Limited edition drops tied to real chess milestones — creates FOMO without pay-to-win

Why ChessX is Different

Anti-Ponzi Design

  • Chess cannot be "won" by cheating for rewards — rewards are engagement-based, not win-based
  • The game remains permanently free for pure learners — no paywall on basic play or AI training
  • NFTs are optional vanity — they never give gameplay advantage
  • Revenue is real (fiat from optional upgrades + cosmetics), not circular token selling

Vision

While every other game has stayed the same price for 20 years (or gone free + ad-supported), ChessX rewards the player's attention the same way Netflix raised prices while increasing value.

The result: higher lifetime engagement, organic virality through "I earned real tokens just by getting better at chess", and a sustainable GameFi revival that actually teaches a skill.

No infinite printing. No win-based rewards. Every number is built to survive audits and grant applications.
