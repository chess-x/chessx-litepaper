Overview

Please read our Notice and Disclaimer before reading this document. This litepaper is subject to change as development progresses.
ChessX is free to play with no staking or wagering of money or money's worth.

ChessX is a free-to-play mobile chess app that turns traditional chess into a lifelong learning platform with light blockchain rewards — without becoming another failed play-to-earn casino.

The entire experience stays web2-smooth: fast matchmaking, no wallet friction for core play. Only two things live on-chain:

  • Collectible NFT chess pieces & AI companions (purely cosmetic — zero impact on gameplay or balance)
  • A single fungible token ($CHX) that rewards real engagement

Core Loop

Every meaningful action in ChessX earns Gems instantly. Gems are capped daily and weekly — no infinite farming.

Play & Earn Gems

ActionGems Earned
Castling+5 Gems
10-minute focused session+20 Gems
Completing daily puzzles+50 Gems
Weekend multiplier2×

Gems → $CHX

At the end of each month, all unspent Gems auto-convert into $CHX and loyalty points.

$CHX → Value

Every fiat upgrade fee triggers an automatic buyback and burn of $CHX. Loyalty points from Gems can be staked for small $CHX yield or used to enter exclusive tournaments, creating continuous demand.

Chess cannot be "won" by cheating for rewards because rewards are engagement-based, not win-based.

How NFTs Support the Token

  • Secondary market royalty: 5% fee on every resale → 100% goes to automatic $CHX buyback & burn
  • Optional staking: Lock any NFT for 30 days → +3% Gem earning boost (capped, no leaderboard impact)

  • Limited edition drops tied to real chess milestones — creates FOMO without pay-to-win

Why ChessX is Different

Anti-Ponzi Design

  • Chess cannot be "won" by cheating for rewards — rewards are engagement-based, not win-based
  • The game remains permanently free for pure learners — no paywall on basic play or AI training
  • NFTs are optional vanity — they never give gameplay advantage
  • Revenue is real (fiat from optional upgrades + cosmetics), not circular token selling

Vision

While every other game has stayed the same price for 20 years (or gone free + ad-supported), ChessX rewards the player's attention the same way Netflix raised prices while increasing value.

The result: higher lifetime engagement, organic virality through "I earned real tokens just by getting better at chess", and a sustainable GameFi revival that actually teaches a skill.

No infinite printing. No win-based rewards. Every number is built to survive audits and grant applications.
