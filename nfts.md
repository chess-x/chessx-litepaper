# NFTs

NFTs give **zero gameplay advantage**. They're collectible chess pieces and AI companions you collect because it feels good — with a light tie-in to the token so they're not dead weight.

All NFTs are on Polygon (ERC-721) and can be earned through pure play, so even free users can get them eventually.

## NFT Tiers

| Tier | Supply | Cost | What You Get |
|---|---|---|---|
| Common Pieces | 60% | Free or 500 Gems | Basic wooden look |
| Rare Pieces | 25% | 2,000 Gems or $4.99 | Shiny metal, animated moves |
| Epic Pieces | 10% | 5,000 Gems or $9.99 | Legendary board themes + custom sound effects |
| Legendary AI Companions | 5% | 10,000 Gems or $19.99 | Visual avatar for your coach (cosmetic only) |

## NFT Utility

| Use Case | How It Works | Why It Feels Cool |
|---|---|---|
| Visual Collection | Display rare pieces on profile & in-game board | Bragging rights |
| Stake for Tiny Gem Boost | Lock NFT 30 days → +3% gems (no leaderboard impact) | Passive reward for collectors |
| Resale & Royalty | Sell on secondary market → 5% royalty auto-burns $LRN | Creates real buy pressure |
| Full-Set Bonus | Complete 16-piece set → one-time 500 Gem drop | Completionist dopamine |
| Limited Edition Events | Own specific drop to unlock special monthly puzzle theme | Feels exclusive |
| Gifting / Trading | Send to friends or trade freely | Community & virality |

## How NFTs Support the Token

- **Secondary market royalty:** 5% fee on every resale → 100% goes to automatic $LRN buyback & burn
- **Optional staking:** Lock any NFT for 30 days → +3% Gem earning boost (capped, no leaderboard impact)
- **Collection bonuses:** Own a full set of 16 pieces → one-time 500 bonus Gems (once per account)
- **Limited edition drops** tied to real chess milestones (e.g., "1 million games played globally") — creates FOMO without pay-to-win
