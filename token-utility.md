# Token Utility

$LRN is **not** the game. It's the optional accelerator for players who want to level up faster or show off.

| Use Case | How It Works | Why Players Care |
|---|---|---|
| Level & AI Coach Upgrades | Spend $LRN to unlock the next player tier (AI strength still 70% learning-based) | Faster progress without waiting |
| Tournament Tickets | Burn or stake $LRN to enter weekend cash-prize events | Real stakes + bragging rights |
| Loyalty Stake | Stake $LRN → up to +25% better Gem → $LRN conversion rate | Turn engagement into more tokens |
| Cosmetic Boosts (non-NFT) | Buy extra board themes or move animations | Pure flex |
| Governance (2027+) | Vote on new puzzle packs, charity chess programs, etc. | Players actually own the direction |
| Gem Top-up (emergency) | Small $LRN → Gem conversion if you're just 50 Gems short of a milestone | Convenience |
